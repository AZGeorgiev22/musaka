Today, robots have reached a significantly advanced level compared to the past. With advancements in science and technology, robotics has made great strides and has reached a point where robots can perform complex tasks and function in various fields. Modern robots are equipped with highly developed sensors, artificial intelligence, and advanced programming systems, allowing them to interact with the environment and perform diverse functions. They can carry out tasks in areas such as industrial automation, medicine, research, construction, automotive industry, delivery services, and many others. Robots are also used in households as helpers for cleaning, cooking, communication, and entertainment. Social robots are emerging as well, designed to provide companionship and assistance to people in their daily activities.

The progress in robotics continues, and in the future, we can expect even more intelligent and autonomous robots that will play a significant role in our daily lives and society as a whole.
